I'm Clementina Chukwu, a data analyst based in Abuja, Nigeria, with a growing specialisation in the oil, gas and energy sector. I build interactive dashboards and performance scorecards that turn raw operational data into insights that actually change decisions.

My journey into analytics started with a practical, self-driven approach learning Power BI, DAX, Excel and Tableau through real projects and building a portfolio that speaks for itself. I create the work, and let the quality do the talking.

"Data should tell a story. Not just show numbers, communicate what they mean, what needs to change, and what to do next."

Alongside building Datara.Insight, I currently serve as Research & Technical Support Officer at the Interior Designers Association of Nigeria (IDAN)

I'm building toward a full-time freelance analytics practice, with a niche in oil and gas sector data, active clients in the Middle East, and a portfolio designed to demonstrate real-world skills across Power BI, Tableau and Excel.
