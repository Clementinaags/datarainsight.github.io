Datara.Insight
Data Analytics Consultancy · Est. 2026

Datara.Insight is a data analytics consultancy founded by Clementina Chukwu, specialising in oil, gas and energy sector analytics. We deliver interactive dashboards, pilot trial performance scorecards and data-driven insights tailored for energy companies across Africa and the Middle East.

We exist because energy companies generate vast amounts of operational data, but that data only becomes valuable when it's presented clearly, interactively, and in a way that drives real decisions. That's what Datara.Insight does.

Our Mission
To make energy sector data accessible, visual and actionable; helping oil and gas companies across Africa and the Middle East unlock the full value of their operational data through world-class dashboard design.
🛢️
Energy Sector Specialisation
Deep, focused expertise in oil, gas and energy analytics not generalist work. We understand production data, well performance metrics, cost structures and field operations.
📊
Interactive Dashboard Design
Power BI and Tableau dashboards built for real operational use, with live slicers, dynamic KPIs, and executive summaries that update automatically.
📋
Pilot Trial Scorecards
Specialised pre and post-treatment scorecards for EOR pilot trials tracking well performance, incremental recovery, and treatment effectiveness over monitoring periods.
🌍
Africa & Middle East Focus
Headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria with an active client in Abu Dhabi, UAE. Serving the fast-growing energy markets of West Africa, the Gulf, and the broader MENA region.
