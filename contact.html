FAQ

Common Questions

Where are you based and can you work remotely?
I'm based in Lagos, Nigeria and work fully remotely. I've already successfully engaged with a client in Abu Dhabi, UAE — distance is not an issue for dashboard projects.
What tools do you deliver dashboards in?
Primarily Power BI and Tableau, with Excel for scorecard deliverables. If you have a specific tool requirement, let me know in your message and we can discuss compatibility.
Can you work with confidential or NDA-protected data?
Yes. The live IIP nanoActiv® project is already protected under a signed NDA. I take confidentiality seriously and am comfortable signing NDAs before project scoping.
Do you only work in oil and gas?
Oil, gas and energy is my primary specialisation, but the skills transfer — I've built financial, supply chain and HSE dashboards too. If your project involves operational data that needs to be visualised clearly, reach out.
